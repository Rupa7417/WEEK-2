ELECTION RESULTS IN ANDHRA PRADESH

By Shweta kumari | Published on June 10, 2024

Andhra Pradesh Assembly Election Result 2024 Updates: TDP wins Assembly elections in Andhra Pradesh, secures 135 seats


The Telugu Desam Party won the Andhra Pradesh Assembly elections by winning 135 seats, while the Jana Sena Party secured 21 seats, the incumbent YSR Congress Party secured 11 seats and the BJP got eight seats.


Pawan Kalyan says time to work for people


Janasena Party chief and actor Pawan Kalyan on Tuesday said his is the only party in the entire country which has won 100 per cent of the seats contested. "This is the day of historic judgment. We have committed to the people of Andhra Pradesh that there will be a responsible government. The NDA government will honour it," he said. The actor-turned-politician also said the NDA will form a responsible government in Andhra Pradesh and the Janasena will ensure all the poll promises it made are honoured.

T20 WORLD CUP

By India today sports desk | Published on June 10, 2024

T20 World Cup: Rohit Sharma's captaincy against Pakistan was all class, says Uthappa


India vs Pakistan, T20 World Cup 2024: Former India batter Robin Uthappa heaped praise on Rohit Sharma's captaincy, saying the skipper trusted his bowlers to get the job done in their successful defence of 119.


In Short * India successfully defended 119 against Pakistan in New York on Sunday * Uthappa lauded Rohit Sharma's handling of the bowling unit *Pakistan are on the verge of an early exit from the tournament


Rohit Sharma was all class with his role as captain and he trusted every bowler to do their job in India's win over Pakistan in a low-scoring T20 World Cup 2024 match on Sunday in New York, according to former India batter Robin Uthappa. India successfully defended 119, the lowest total they have managed to defend, to hand Pakistan a stinging blow. Bowlers saved the day for India after their star batters failed to get going on a ticky pitch at the Nassau County Cricket Stadium in New York.
